Quick Start Steps
1
Charge the Adaptor
Connect the USB-C cable to the charging port of the adaptor for charging to start.
2
Pour water into the Bottle
Remove the currently fitted top and Pour your drinking water into the bottle.
3
Fix the adaptor to the bottle
Fix the Hydrofit adaptor to the bottle by rotating it in clockwise direction.
4
Set the bottle on a Flat surface
Set the Bottle on a flat surface for a few seconds so that the adaptor can begin calibration and measure the water level. To get accurate measurements set the bottle on a flat surface after every drink and refilling.

Display Information

The LCD display shows water consumption in milliliters and current time. The touch-sensitive areas on both sides allow you to control all functions with simple tap gestures. The display automatically dims after 10 seconds of inactivity to conserve battery.
